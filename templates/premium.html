How does the real shell environment work?

Our premium subscription gives you access to secure containerized Linux and Windows environments where you can practice any command safely. These environments are isolated, reset after each session, and provide a realistic terminal experience directly in your browser.

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time. If you cancel within the first 30 days, you'll receive a full refund. After that, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

Are there team or organization plans available?

How secure is the real shell environment?

Our shell environments are highly secured through container isolation, network restrictions, and automatic session timeouts. Each user gets their own dedicated environment that's completely isolated from other users and resets after each session for maximum security.
